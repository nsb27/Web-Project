Friday

Friday we believe is all about unwinding after the work week and starting your weekend in style.
So, we have rounded up some suggestions on the best way to enjoy your evening from a sophisticated cocktail to late night where you can let your hair down dancing.


Have a great evening

GORDON'S WINE BAR

With a cosy indoors and outdoor seating near Embankment station, this bar is great for wine and cheese lovers looking to unwind after the work week!

ROADHOUSE

This American-diner style bar in Covent Garden is great for casual drinks and Friday night dancing if you’re in the mood!

HAPPINESS FORGETS

Hidden in Hoxton Square, this tiny cocktail bar guarantees a classy evening to unwind with friends.

HOPE AND ANCHOR

With iconic outdoor garden huts and a massive screen, this South London pub guarantees a fun night!

BARSMITH

Unwind after the working week with 2 for 1 cocktails until 9pm at this Farringdon staple.

EVANS AND PEEL DETECTIVE AGENCY

This gorgeous speakeasy in Earls Court is great for something a bit different to end the work week!

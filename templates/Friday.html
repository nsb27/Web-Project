Our Friday night recomendations


Friday we believe is all about unwinding after the work week and starting your weekend in style. So, we have rounded up some suggestions on the best way to enjoy your evening from a sophisticated cocktail to late night where you can let your hair down dancing.

ENJOY:

Barsmith

Unwind after the working week with 2 for 1 cocktails until 9pm at this Farringdon staple.

When to go: When you need to let your hair down after a long week

 Check out their website

Hope and Anchor

With iconic outdoor garden huts and a massive screen, this South London pub guarantees a fun night!

When to go: As a stop on a night out with your pals

Check out their website

RoadHouse

This American-diner style bar in Covent Garden is great for casual drinks and Friday night dancing if you’re in the mood!

When to go: Night out with the crew

Check out their website

Happiness Forgets

Hidden in Hoxton Square, this tiny cocktail bar guarantees a classy evening to unwind with friends.

When to go: For a classy night with someone special

Check out their website

Gordon’s Wine Bar

When to go: Looking for a chilled night with wine to end the workweek

With a cosy indoors and outdoor seating near Embankment station, this bar is great for wine and cheese lovers looking to unwind after the work week!

Check out their website

Evans and Peel Detective Agency

This gorgeous speakeasy in Earls Court is great for something a bit different to end the work week!

When to go: for an evening of fun sophistication
